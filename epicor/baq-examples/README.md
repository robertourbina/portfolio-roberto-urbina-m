# Business Activity Queries (BAQs)

Welcome to the **Business Activity Queries (BAQs)** section of my Epicor ERP portfolio.

Business Activity Queries (BAQs) are one of the most powerful features of Epicor ERP, enabling organizations to retrieve, analyze, and present business information without directly modifying the database. Well-designed BAQs provide meaningful insights that support operational efficiency, informed decision-making, and continuous business improvement.

This repository contains a growing collection of BAQ implementations based on real-world business scenarios commonly found in manufacturing organizations. Each implementation demonstrates not only the technical solution, but also the business analysis, functional requirements, and implementation process that led to the final result.

The objective of this section is to showcase my experience as an **Epicor ERP Application Specialist**, highlighting my ability to understand business processes, translate business requirements into practical ERP solutions, and document each implementation following professional consulting standards.
